About Accessible Design Co.

We are a tiny studio that likes sticky notes, coffee that is probably too strong, and shipping projects that don’t leave anyone behind.

Inclusive by default

We run every sketch by screen readers, color contrast checkers, and our friend Sam who always finds the one button nobody else noticed.

Human-centered process

We interview, doodle, test, throw things out, then test again until the flow just feels right. Nobody wants to fight a form at 2 AM.

Measurable outcomes

Cleaner analytics, happier customers, fewer legal headaches. Toss in a calmer support inbox too.
